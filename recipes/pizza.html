How to Make Pizza

  • Summon the Pizza Genie: Rub an ancient pizza stone and chant, "Cheesus Crusticus," hoping for magical toppings.
  • Dough Dance-off: Challenge your pizza dough to a dance-off for a fluffier crust. Play dough-friendly tunes.
  • Tomato Sauce Tango: Teach your tomato sauce to tango in the bowl for a tastier experience.
  • Cheese Avalanche: Shred mountains of cheese for a dramatic avalanche effect on your pizza.
  • Toppling Toppings Tower: Create a Jenga-style tower of toppings for a playful and precarious arrangement.
  • Pepperoni Constellation: Arrange pepperoni in the shape of a constellation for a cheesy cosmic story.
